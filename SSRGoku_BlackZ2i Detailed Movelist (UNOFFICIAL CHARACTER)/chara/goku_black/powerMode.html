Activate Superior Mode
Foolish mortals! Daring to face a god!! The gloves are figuratively off, and Black is now taking you seriously, his newfound Saiyan biology relishing the challenge! While the mode is active, Black receives quite a few changes
- Black glows with overwhelming energy!
- Blacks overall meter gain is reduced
- Pushback on Blacks normals are altered
- All of Blacks Normals gain additional frames of hitstun. Sounds small, but opens up a lot of possibilties, and makes him a potent threat!
- He gains access to a new "Superior Gauge" that he can utilise in various ways. It always starts fully stocked
- Dependant on how much Superior Gauge he has, Black deals additional damage
- Access to new moves, including multiple specials and even a Super serving as a mode exit!
- Altered Super properties (KMH launches higher, Rapid Heel Barrage deals more hits, etc)

Backfist Chain
Black is beginning to truly make Gokus power his own! after a close Medium Kick, Black backfists the opponent similar to Goku, with a healthy amount of frame advantage to boot!
Hurricane Heel
Following the slash, Black can cancel in to a spinning kick upon making contact with the opponent. This kicks packs a punch, and sends the enemy flying! You can also teleport after the opponent by pressing Up/Up and any direction. Can also be done outside of Emo as a dash attack with Heavy Kick.
Elusive Truth
Black's backdash now becomes a quick teleport, invincible on startup. Good for escaping oppressive situations, but can be punished if made too obvious, and gives up a fair bit of screen space
Clone Rush
Black's bringin the pain, and he has a clone to back him up! Black sends a clone out in front of him to assault the opponent, laying in to them with a dozen punches. Great for pressure and extensions, but keep in mind that any use with 2 or less bars of SUPERIOR Gauge will drain what remains, meaning you can only use two at most.
Clone Trip
Don't take Blacks pressure lightly! By spawning a clone to sweep the opponent, Black can continue to assault the opponent. The move is also Special and Super cancellable
Profound Foresight
You can't hurt Black, and he knows it! Black raises his hand to his head, practically begging his opponent to hit him. Once they take the bait, Black parries the attack, allowing you to get the upper hand on a predicatble opponent, as well as restoring a bar of his Superior Gauge!
Overhead Divination
A clone joins Black on the assault, coming out in front him. This clone goes for an overhead kick, leaving Black free to push his offense. In addition, by instead holding the MP+MK input, Black gains a defensive wall where the clone is, as it will attack anything that comes near it. In addition to this, pressing MP+MK again while the clone is standing in the air will make it do a lunging attack, giving black many plus frames if it connects.
He can also do a ground version of this clone set by holding DB or Down and either pressing or holding MP+MK.
The ground clone will not attack the opponent when they are close and the setup move is only able to be cancelled into from 2HK. In return though, it becomes much easier to press advantage with the clone's lunging attack after the clone has been placed alongside using moves like 6MP to press deadly mixups.
Spirit God Impact (LVL 3)
Exclusive to Superior mode, Black twists towards the opponent, transitioning in to a cinematic on hit. Black charges his own Genkidama. the resulting energy being blasted in to a huge explosion on to the enemy. Fully invincible, and packing one heck of a wallop! Even capable of destroying the opponent on match win
