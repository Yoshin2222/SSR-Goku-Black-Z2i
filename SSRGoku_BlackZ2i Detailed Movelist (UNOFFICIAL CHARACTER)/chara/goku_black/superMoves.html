Kamehameha (LVL 1)
Identical to Gokus in every way, aside from a few notable differences. This attack launches the opponent further up while in EMO mode, allowing for extensions. It also doesn't have an Up version, so its primarily a combo ender/fireball war tool, and is pretty useless as an anti air.
Air Kamehameha (LVL 1)
Identical to Gokus in every way, aside from a few- wait i've said this before. Another carried over super, but with a fun twist. The opponent is now blasted in to the air, allowing you to further extend. It can also be cancelled in to from Elbow drop, leading to some oppressive pressure.
Shippu Jinrai Kyakku (LVL 1)
SF3 Ken called, and wants his Super back! Taking obvious hints from said Super, Black performs a series of rotating kicks, followed up by a Tatsu that launches the opponent off the ground. Black regains control while in this state, and can cancel from these kicks at any point. Mess around!
Eternal Uniter (LVL 2)
Ragna's gonna sue! Black charges up a massive scythe attack, that reaches almost full screen in front of him, with the superpause allowing it to come out functionally in a couple of frames. Great for punishes, and certain extensions where other moves would fail. Massive thank you to JARMS31 for spriting this move!
True Nocturne (LVL 1)
Taking cues from a similar super, Black channels his inner Remy with a counter super. Potentially unsafer than Z Counter, but substantially more rewarding! Can be done while guarding
Rapid Heel Barrage (LVL 2)
Black unleashes a flurry of kicks, capable of being extend either by linking from it, or by holding a punch button, where he extends by slashing the opponent up, before slicing them back down. Very fun to mess around with, and pretty consistent in and out of juggles, go wild and see what you can do with it!
Divine Rush (LVL 3)
A Cinematic Level 3! Black crosses up the opponent, punching the opponent in the gut, going in to a Cinematic follow up on hit. Black ruthlessly attacks the opponent, the followup changing depending on how much Superior Gauge he has
-SUPERIOR VERSIONS-
- (2) - Bursting Rose
After delivering a powerful uppercut to the opponents gut, Black adds insult to injury by delivering a second one, before kicking them away, charging and then blasting them with a huge Kamehameha. He taunts them, before punching them to ground, catching them with a rising knee attack before slicing them down!
- (3) - Mirrors Edge
Black slices the opponent with multiple clones dashing in from both sides. taking inspiration from Dragon Ball FighterZ, Black finishes the attack with a devestating slice downards, obliterating the opponent if he wins the match!
Truth Barrage (LVL 3)
Taking some notes from Kim Kaphwan, Black lunges towards the opponent, unleashing an onslaught of kicks, finishing in a kick so forceful it causes them to be slammed on to the ground! Invulnerable to Projectiles, so keep that in mind. Also good to note is that Black only kicks when he's close.
Resentful Angel (LVL 3)
Obvious Akuma reference is obvious! Black has a Raging Demon, with a unique twist. This move is considered a Hit Grab, so its possible to combo in to it, even has the classic pose if you kill the opponent with it!
