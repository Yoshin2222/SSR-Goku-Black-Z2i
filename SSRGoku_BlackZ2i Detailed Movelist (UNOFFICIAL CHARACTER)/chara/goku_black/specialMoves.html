Justice Cross
A command normal Black can use for more range but less safety compared to f.HP. It also can be used to kara throw by taking advantage of the forward movement.
Hurricane Heel
While dashing, Black cancels from it to unleash spiining kick to the opponent. This kicks packs a punch, and sends the enemy flying! You can also teleport after the opponent by pressing Up/Up and any direction
Ascension Phase
While in the air, Black can teleport directly upwards. This teleport can also be cancelled in to as if it were a\ speciall move, and can lead to some tricky situations with the right know how. Definitely worth testing
Second Impact
Black can cancel from his light kick into his Light Punch in the air
Twisted Reverse
By holding Back while airborne, Black will twist around, kicking in the opposite direction. Great for cross ups, and catching a fleeing opponent behind you!
Elbow Drop
After jumping forward, Black raises his Elbow and brings it down for an almost unreactable overhead. The move is nothing special on hit, but it has an intended use. Its active for quite a while, making it a decent meaty attack. Due to its active frames, using it as a meaty will give it enough plus frames for a full combo conversion, making the move scary to defend against in wakeup situations.
Ki Blast
Similarly to Goku, Black fires a Ki Blast from his palms, with it always juggling, once again similar to Goku. The fireball goes different speeds depending on the strength of button pressed. It also can be repeated up to two times using Forward and the strength of the Fireball you want to shoot. You can have a maximum of 3, and the enemy can be hit with 5 before falling. The EX Version fires 3 Light speed shots, which don't add to the fireball limit/count. The EX version can be done after a starting fireball shot and after any followup using Forward and any two Punches.
Rising Justice
Thanks to R3DGrave for initially programming this move! Black swings upwards. kicking the opponent away on hit! While holding up, Black will follow the opponent similar to how he does in his HP>HK Target Combo. Can lead to devestating extensions, but beware is massively unsafe on block. Keep in mind, this move can only be performed by cancelling in to it from an attack
Righteous Flurry
This move is pretty interesting. Every strength has a different version, and even the EX is unique. Every version starts with Black performing a spin kick, then transitions in to the following attacks. The Light variant has him kick an additional two times, with each moving him forward. It's also the only version thats Super Cancellable, and hits enemies off the ground. The Medium version (as shown in the image) has Black kick the opponent upwards, in a fashion akin to CR.HP, only that the enemy can't tech out of the airborne state. The Heavy version has Black kick the opponent to the ground, and while it does the most damage, keep in mind that it affects juggling similarly to his A.HK, which ground bounces. The EX version was inspired by SF3 Akumas SA3, Messatsu Gorasen. Black quickly spins in the air, pulling the opponent in and kicking them repeatedly in quick succession, with him ending it with a brutal smash to the ground! Pretty handy in long combos since it'll do a bare minimum of 49 damage! In addition, all versions can be cancelled into from Light, Medium, and Heavy Fireball.
Air Tatsumaki
Referencing a certain red gi wearing Shotokan, Black spins and kicks in the air, with the EX version spawning a clone. You can even steer the clone by pressing directions. Try it out and see what you can come up with!
Savage Dive
Black falls quickly with his leg extended, knocking the opponent down on hit. You can juggle out of this knockdown, especially useful when cancelled in tofrom air attacks on a juggled opponent. The EX version, on a standing opponent, leads to Black performing an attack similar to Savage Kick from DragonBall FighterZ. This state only adds 1 to scaling, even though he attacks twice. This additional state can also be activated after his LVL2 Super, Rapid Hell Barrage.
